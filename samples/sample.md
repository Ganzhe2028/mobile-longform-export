# A Small Mixed-Language Sample

This is a synthetic sample for testing Mobile Longform Export. It contains
English words, numbers like 2026, and Chinese text in the same paragraph.

## Why mobile pages matter

移动端阅读不是把桌面 PDF 缩小。读者拿着手机时，行宽、行高、字距和页边距都会直接影响阅读速度。

For a long article, the export should keep the text calm, readable, and easy to
scan. "Straight quotes" should become proper quotation marks, and long English
words such as internationalization should not break into unreadable fragments.
